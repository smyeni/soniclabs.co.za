# Sonic Labs (Pty) Ltd

Welcome to **Sonic Labs**, our innovation playground and web development university.  
This is where we experiment, learn, and share — openly and transparently.

---

## 🌐 Public Innovation
Sonic Labs is dedicated to:
- Web development experiments and proof‑of‑concepts
- Educational writeups and troubleshooting guides
- Showcasing production‑ready workflows and modular code
- Building a culture of learning and knowledge sharing

All experiments here are safe for public exposure. They demonstrate capability, creativity, and technical discipline.

---

## 🔒 Private Security
While Sonic Labs is open, our **RF and defense‑related projects** are strictly private:
- Antenna design and RF simulations
- Defense industry R&D
- Sensitive IP under NDA and compliance frameworks

These projects are hosted separately on secure, private infrastructure.  
We never mix sensitive work with public experiments — ensuring clear boundaries and audit‑ready compliance.

---

## ⚖️ Philosophy
**Public innovation, private security.**  
We believe in sharing knowledge to build trust, while protecting sensitive IP with rigor.  
This dual‑track approach reassures clients and partners: we experiment openly, but we innovate responsibly.
